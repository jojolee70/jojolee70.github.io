Condor Light

Created in 2010 by David Jonathan Ross, Condor is a typeface that cleverly plays on thick and thin weighted slabs while including the classy detail of flattened curves. This typeface is typically used on landmarks or in architectural settings; however,for me it is much more classy than given credit for. It offers strong fashion ties with companies like Armani and Bloomingdale’s. Condor Light is a string typeface that does just enough to get your attention, but won’t beg for it- like that pretty girl in high school hallways on movies like Mean Girls. Yes, I did just refer to Condor Light as Regina George at least before Cady Heron and the whole bus situation.
